Aluminum Mesh Filter Service

appliance aluminum mesh filter service maintains the primary grease capture system in most range hoods. These multi-layer aluminum filters trap grease particles before they reach the blower motor and ductwork. Clogged mesh filters reduce airflow, increase motor strain, and create fire hazards from grease buildup. We professionally clean mesh filters using commercial degreasers, inspect for damage or warping, and replace filters that no longer capture effectively. Regular mesh filter service extends hood life and maintains optimal ventilation.

Baffle Filter Cleaning & Replacement

appliance baffle filter maintenance services the professional-grade stainless steel filters found in high-performance hoods. Baffle filters use angled channels to change airflow direction, causing grease to separate and drain into collection trays. These filters handle heavier cooking loads than mesh alternatives. We clean baffle filters, clear drainage channels, replace worn baffles, and ensure proper seating. Commercial kitchens and serious home cooks rely on baffle filter efficiency for effective ventilation.

Charcoal Filter Replacement

appliance charcoal filter replacement restores odor removal in ductless and recirculating range hoods. Charcoal (activated carbon) filters absorb cooking odors that grease filters cannot capture. Unlike washable grease filters, charcoal filters require periodic replacement‚Äîtypically every 3-6 months depending on cooking frequency. We supply genuine OEM charcoal filters, remove spent filters, and install fresh replacements. Proper charcoal filter maintenance keeps your kitchen smelling fresh when exterior ducting isn't possible.

Filter Housing & Seal Repair

appliance filter housing repair fixes the frames, latches, and seals that secure filters in position. Damaged housing allows unfiltered air to bypass the filter system, reducing efficiency and depositing grease on internal components. We repair bent filter frames, replace broken latches, restore filter seals, and ensure airtight filter installation. Proper filter housing function is essential‚Äîeven the best filter cannot work if air flows around it rather than through it.
